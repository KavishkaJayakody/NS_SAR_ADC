1. Primary Accent — “Silicon Red”

Used for: EDGE text + the dynamic incision pointer detail in the logo

HEX: #C8102E

Why: Strong, industry-aligned engineering grade, not flashy marketing red.

⚫ 2. Core Background — “Deep Silicon Navy”

Used for: Main background for branding, slides, and white papers

HEX: #0A1A2F

Why: More sophisticated and gives better contrast than plain black.

⚪ 3. Signal White — “Analog White”

Used for: Waveform and SILICON text

HEX: #E6E8EB

Why: A softened, scientific white that reduces glare on dark backgrounds.

⚙️ 4. IC Gray — “Silicon Material”

Used for: The chip block

HEX: #8A8F98

Why: A neutral, material-like color that looks realistic in flat design.

The key Usage Rule for this palette is to maintain a controlled balance of 70% Navy, 20% White/Gray, and a MAX of 10% Red, keeping the red rare and impactful.
